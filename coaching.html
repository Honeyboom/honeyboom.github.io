Life Coaching

If we asked you to describe a perfect day, what would it include for you? Working on your own successful business? Hiking in the Nepalese mountains or snorkelling in the shores of a Thai island? Completing the first chapter of that book you’ve been wanting to write for years but never had the time?

Whatever dreams make you jump with excitement—even if it’s just having more free time to spend resting and doing nothing—it’s in your power to make it come true.

Think about it this way: if you live up to the age of 90, that means you have at your disposal, from the day you were born until the day you die, a total or 32,850 days to spend. If you are 25, that means that you have 23,725 until you reach that age. If you’re 52, 13,870. As the sun rises each morning, that number goes down by one, whether you want it or not. That’s out of your control.

However, what is entirely in your hands is what you choose to do with each of those precious days. Do you choose to spend them in bored despair, doing a job you hate and hoping that one day things will magically change? Or do you choose to feel grateful and excited, fulfilled by a life of purpose and passion—and begin the journey to achieve that?
